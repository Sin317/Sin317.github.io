Babycorn Palya

How to make babycorn palya with kurma style

Ingredients (4 servings)#

  1. Babycorn (1 trader joes pack)
  2. Cauliflower (half)
  3. 1.5 capsicum
  4. 1 onion (medium size)
  5. 1 tomato
  6. 4 green chilly
  7. 1 twig curry leaves
  8. whole spices (3 cardamom, 0.5 cinamon, 2 cloves, 1 bay leaf)
  9. kasuri methi
  10. garam masala - 1 or 2 spoons
  11. palya powder - 1 spoon
  12. coriander - 2 spoons
  13. cumin - 1 spoon
  14. salt - 2 spoons
  15. red chili - 1 spoon

Recipe#

Prep#

  1. Cut babycorn into slices diagonally
  2. Cut cauliflower
  3. Cut capsicum vertically
  4. Cut onions
  5. Cut green chilly

Cook#

  1. Bring oil to hot
  2. Add mustard and whole spices
  3. Add onion, green chilly and stir
  4. Add ginger garlic paste
  5. Add tomato
  6. Add spices - turmeric, cumin, coriander, kashmiri red chili, red chili, garam masala, salt
  7. Add curry leaves
  8. let it cook on medium heat for 5mins
  9. Turn off flame and let it cool
  10. In another pan add the veggies - cauliflower, capsicum, babycorn on stir fry for 5-10mins (use butter also)
  11. Grind the onion tomato mixture to fine paste
  12. Bring it to heat and let it cook
  13. Add the stir fried veggies
  14. Let it cook for 5 mins
  15. Add the heavy cream and kasuri methi for garnish and taste test.
