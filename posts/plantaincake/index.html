Plantain Cake Making

Ingredients#

For serving size of 1 cake in 9x9 diameter cake

  1. 2 ripe plantains
  2. 2 cups flour
  3. 1 tsp Salt
  4. 1 Tsp baking powder
  5. 1/4 Tsp baking soda
  6. 3/4 cup sugar
  7. 8 tbsp butter or 1 stick butter
  8. 1/4 cup Yogurt
  9. 2 eggs
  10. 3 tbsp milk
  11. 1 tsp vanilla extract

Recipe#

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F, then grease and flour the baking pan and set aside.
  2. Combine the flour, salt, and baking powder and baking soda.
  3. Whisk, mash or blend the plantains till fairly smooth.
  4. Use a hand whisk to cream the butter and sugar for 2 to 3 minutes. Add the eggs one at a time and cream together until just combined. Add the Yogurt and vanilla and whisk together.
  5. Mix the mashed plantains with the creamed butter and add the mixture to the dry ingredients (flour, salt, and baking powder). Fold a couple of times until well mixed. (don’t over mix).
  6. Stir in the milk and mix till combined.
  7. Mix chocolate chips, some nuts such as almonds, cashews, walnuts for crunchy texture (optional)
  8. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan. Smooth out the surface of the batter and bake for 50 minutes to 1 hr or till a tester or toothpick inserted into the middle of the cake comes out clean.
  9. Let the cake cool down and then cut it and serve!
